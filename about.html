Our Mission

At Happy Tails Haven, our mission is simple: to rescue, rehabilitate, and rehome abandoned, abused, and neglected dogs and cats. We strive to provide a safe and loving environment for every animal that comes through our doors, ensuring they receive the care and attention they need to thrive.

Our Story

Happy Tails Haven was founded in 2024 by a group of passionate animal lovers who saw the urgent need for a dedicated rescue and shelter facility in our community. Over the years, we have grown from a small, volunteer-run organization to a well-established haven for dogs and cats in need.
